Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase items from the shop?

Browse our shop items, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept various payment methods including PayPal and credit cards.

How do I join the Discord server?

Click the "Join Discord Server" button on our homepage or use the invite link: discord.gg/iraq. You'll be able to access exclusive channels and features.

What are Discord roles and how do I get them?

Discord roles provide special permissions and access to exclusive channels. You can purchase roles from our shop or earn them through community participation.

How do I get a refund?

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase for most items. Contact our support team with your order details to initiate a refund request.

Are the voice channels private?

Some voice channels are exclusive to specific roles. You can purchase access to private channels or earn access through community participation.
