Refund Policy

Our policy on refunds and cancellations

General Refund Policy

At Iraq Cafe Shop, we strive to provide the best digital products and services. We understand that sometimes a purchase may not meet your expectations. Here's our refund policy:

Eligible for Refunds

The following items are eligible for refunds within 7 days of purchase:

  • Discord roles that haven't been assigned yet
  • Voice channel access that hasn't been activated
  • Gaming tournament entries (before the tournament starts)
  • Premium content that hasn't been accessed
  • Technical issues preventing service delivery

Not Eligible for Refunds

The following items are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Discord roles that have already been assigned
  • Voice channel access that has been activated
  • Gaming tournament entries after the tournament has started
  • Premium content that has been accessed or downloaded
  • Services that have been fully delivered
  • Purchases made more than 7 days ago

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team within 7 days of your purchase
  2. Provide your order details (order number, date of purchase)
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Include your Discord username if applicable

Refund Processing

Once we receive your refund request:

  • We will review your request within 24-48 hours
  • If approved, we will process the refund to your original payment method
  • Refunds typically take 3-5 business days to appear in your account
  • You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed

Special Circumstances

We may consider refunds in special circumstances:

  • Technical issues on our end preventing service delivery
  • Duplicate purchases due to system errors
  • Unauthorized transactions
  • Service unavailability due to maintenance or updates
